## 2018 Fall Canton Fair Begins: A Must-Not-Miss Opportunity for Export-Oriented Companies

For export-oriented enterprises, the 2018 fall Canton Fair was an indispensable opportunity. Typically, companies would showcase their latest products in the most prominent positions at their booths. Among them, there were some interesting applications to be found.

### Lekue Electrical Appliances

Lekue Electrical Appliances usually gives off a sense of being somewhat perfunctory when it comes to exhibitions. At this fair, they displayed some machines rather casually. The main machine showcased at the exhibition was a coffee maker!!!
