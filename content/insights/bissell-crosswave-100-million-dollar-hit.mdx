If you ask friends at Bissell what product has been most successful in recent years, 100% of them will tell you it is Crosswave. It is said that this single model sold hundreds of thousands of units, generated more than USD 100 million in sales, helped Bissell successfully break through USD 1 billion in revenue, and entered the “1 Billion Club.”

![Bissell Crosswave: A USD 100 Million Hit Product image 1](/images/insights/bissell-crosswave-100-million-dollar-hit-image-001.jpg)

This product is said to have been developed by the Chinese team for the China market. Bissell CEO Mark Bissell was once not optimistic about this model. But unexpectedly, it became a big hit.

The following description comes from Bissell's Taobao flagship store.

Core function: vacuuming and mopping in one.

![Bissell Crosswave: A USD 100 Million Hit Product image 2](/images/insights/bissell-crosswave-100-million-dollar-hit-image-002.jpg)

Making the floor clean after cleaning requires a water pump to precisely control water output.

![Bissell Crosswave: A USD 100 Million Hit Product image 3](/images/insights/bissell-crosswave-100-million-dollar-hit-image-003.jpg)

It is for both dry and wet use, and can also suck in large particles. I remember Bissell's promotional video used it to suck up raw eggs and noodles.

![Bissell Crosswave: A USD 100 Million Hit Product image 4](/images/insights/bissell-crosswave-100-million-dollar-hit-image-004.jpg)

Brushroll self-cleaning. The biggest problem with electric mop products is that cleaning the mop cloth or roller after use is a pain point, so I think self-cleaning will become standard for mop-type products in the future.

![Bissell Crosswave: A USD 100 Million Hit Product image 5](/images/insights/bissell-crosswave-100-million-dollar-hit-image-005.jpg)

The dirty-water tank and clean-water tank are separated, and dirty water directly enters the dirty-water tank.

![Bissell Crosswave: A USD 100 Million Hit Product image 6](/images/insights/bissell-crosswave-100-million-dollar-hit-image-006.jpg)

A simple product description.

![Bissell Crosswave: A USD 100 Million Hit Product image 7](/images/insights/bissell-crosswave-100-million-dollar-hit-image-007.jpg)

![Bissell Crosswave: A USD 100 Million Hit Product image 8](/images/insights/bissell-crosswave-100-million-dollar-hit-image-008.jpg)

There is no doubt that from a product perspective, Crosswave is a successful product. From early market research to product-function definition, it hit customer pain points. But this product, originally built for the China market, did not sell best in China. Bissell has many patents around this type of product, namely water-based machines, and it is very difficult to avoid them.

I briefly looked at Amazon, and sales were very good.

![Bissell Crosswave: A USD 100 Million Hit Product image 9](/images/insights/bissell-crosswave-100-million-dollar-hit-image-009.jpg)

I looked at negative reviews. They mainly focused on several areas.

1. The power cord is inconvenient. It is said that a cordless version will be released soon.

2. Suction is too weak.

![Bissell Crosswave: A USD 100 Million Hit Product image 10](/images/insights/bissell-crosswave-100-million-dollar-hit-image-010.jpg)

3. Quality is poor and there is water leakage.

![Bissell Crosswave: A USD 100 Million Hit Product image 11](/images/insights/bissell-crosswave-100-million-dollar-hit-image-011.jpg)

4. Some users feel the cleaning effect is not good enough.

![Bissell Crosswave: A USD 100 Million Hit Product image 12](/images/insights/bissell-crosswave-100-million-dollar-hit-image-012.jpg)

5. Some users feel it is noisy.

![Bissell Crosswave: A USD 100 Million Hit Product image 13](/images/insights/bissell-crosswave-100-million-dollar-hit-image-013.jpg)

6. Some users returned it because it did not meet expectations. This point deserves special attention when doing overseas markets. Many overseas consumers use a product for a few days, and if they feel it does not meet their requirements, they return it.

![Bissell Crosswave: A USD 100 Million Hit Product image 14](/images/insights/bissell-crosswave-100-million-dollar-hit-image-014.jpg)

In addition, Bissell's marketing strategy for this product emphasizes that it can pick up pet hair. Many buyers therefore purchased the product for that reason. As a result, many reviews also mention that dog hair cannot be cleaned thoroughly, that it often clogs, and similar issues.
