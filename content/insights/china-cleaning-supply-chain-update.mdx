## China remains a major sourcing base

China continues to be one of the most important sourcing bases for cleaning equipment, components, and product development. The question for global buyers is no longer whether to source from China, but how to find the right type of supplier.

Different factories may be strong in OEM production, ODM development, private label support, components, or channel-specific packaging.

## Common buyer mistakes

- Comparing suppliers only by unit price
- Ignoring certification and compliance needs
- Underestimating after-sales and spare parts requirements
- Choosing a product before defining market positioning
- Working with suppliers that do not understand the target channel

## A better sourcing approach

Start with the buyer's market, channel, customer segment, and product role. Then match supplier type, technical capability, MOQ, customization needs, and long-term support.
