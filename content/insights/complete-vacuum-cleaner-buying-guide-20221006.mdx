b.vacuum cleaner industry introduction

When I wrote the article a few years ago, not many people paid attention to the vacuum cleaner, so at that time, I still needed to popularize a lot of basic knowledge. Introduction to c.vacuum cleaner types


Vacuum cleaners are mainly divided into horizontal vacuum cleaners, vertical vacuum cleaners, push rod vacuum cleaners, barrel vacuum cleaners, cordless handheld vacuum cleaners, robot vacuums, and hard floor washers. Robot vacuums and hard floor washers have relatively large changes in technology and layout, so I will write about them separately later.


Horizontal vacuum cleaner: The previous mainstream vacuum cleaner outside the North American market. Most of the vacuum cleaners we exported to Europe were this type of vacuum cleaner. The characteristic of this vacuum cleaner is that it is corded and cheap. When we exported in the past, the ex-factory price was mostly more than 10-20 US dollars, and the foreign retail price was about 20-40 US dollars. Because it was not convenient to use, in the era of cordless vacuum cleaners, it has been gradually replaced by wireless in the market. The domestic market has basically eliminated these vacuum cleaners. Cleaner. Here I will explain it to you as a knowledge point without introducing too much. If you want to buy this type of vacuum cleaner, the price should not exceed 300 yuan.


Trivia: Since the start of the Russo-Ukrainian war this year, Europeans’ spending has increased significantly. Expensive vacuum cleaners can no longer be sold. This type of cheap horizontal vacuum cleaners have begun to increase in volume again.


Vertical vacuum cleaner: Vertical vacuum cleaner is called Upright vacuum cleaner in English. It is relatively large and is mainly marketed in countries such as North America, the United Kingdom, and Australia. This type of vacuum cleaner has never been popular in our country. Because it is relatively large and inconvenient to use, here we will only explain the knowledge points, and it is not recommended to buy and use it.


Push rod vacuum cleaner: The push rod vacuum cleaner is a simple version of the vertical vacuum cleaner. It is cheap and relatively small in size. When we exported in the past, the price was only about 10 US dollars. The advantage of the small push rod is that it is cheap and small in size.

Bucket-type vacuum cleaner: Bucket-type vacuum cleaner should actually be regarded as a kind of power tool, because it can be used with drills, etc., and its usage scenarios are also at construction sites and factories. In our country, the main usage scenarios of bucket-type vacuum cleaners are: rural villas, factories, and offices. Many people buy bucket-type vacuum cleaners for land reclamation. First, the price is relatively cheap, and the second-bucket vacuum cleaner has a large capacity.


Cordless handheld vacuum cleaner (pistol type): This cordless handheld vacuum cleaner mainly refers to the handheld vacuum cleaner released by Dyson, from the earliest V6, V8 to the later V12, etc. The classic V8-shaped machine is also the most imitated model on the market. In fact, before Dyson, Electrolux’s cordless upright vacuum cleaners sold reasonably well, but why are there no Electrolux models at all? The main reason is that Dyson has released a high-speed digital motor (generally called a brushless motor in China), which can reach 80,000 rpm at the beginning (but other vacuum cleaner motors can only reach about 20,000 rpm). Therefore, Dyson has led the era of high-speed motors. After all the Chinese manufacturers started to follow up, the entire industry began to completely turn to Dyson. We will also focus on recommending these vacuum cleaners.


Mite remover: The mite remover was first invented by South Korea's Raycop. A UV lamp is added to the vacuum cleaner to sterilize. But in fact, the UV lamp has no effect. The main function is to beat the motor and vacuum motor. In other words, use an ordinary cordless vacuum. The cleaner can also play the role of a mite remover. So is the mite remover an IQ tax? It doesn't count. 1. The price of the mite remover is relatively cheap, usually 2-300. Although the ultraviolet lamp does not have much effect, people do not have to pay extra. 2. The mite remover is equivalent to a vacuum cleaner dedicated to the bed, which increases the frequency of cleaning everyone's bed.


Nowadays, many brands have launched double-barrel mite removers. In fact, there is no difference between the double-barrel and ordinary single-barrel mite removers. There is no need to spend more money to buy a double-barrel mite remover.


In the early years, we sold cordless handheld vacuum cleaners, but they were limited by motors and batteries. The suction power of the early cordless handheld vacuum cleaners was very weak, and the battery life was also very poor. It basically took about 10 hours to charge and could be used for 5 minutes... which was very useless. Later, in the era of high-speed brushless motors, coupled with high-performance lithium batteries, the portable vacuum cleaner appeared. The suction power was relatively strong and could be used casually.


Car-mounted vacuum cleaner: Car-mounted vacuum cleaner is also a small subdivision of vacuum cleaner. There are two main power sources. 1. Plug in the cigarette lighter. 2. Use lithium batteries.

Horizontal vacuum cleaner: This is not recommended. If you must buy it, it is recommended that the price does not exceed 300 yuan. Early horizontal vacuum cleaners such as Dyson, Miele, and Vorwerk cost several thousand yuan. Now they are not as convenient to use as cordless handheld models.


Vertical vacuum cleaner: It is large in size and very inconvenient to use. Basically, there are no brands producing this kind of vertical vacuum cleaner in China. It is not recommended to buy it.


Push rod vacuum cleaner: Generally, the motor does not exceed 400-600W, with wires, and the price does not exceed 200 yuan. Basically, the performance is not much different in all aspects. Key points for selection: the price is below 200, choose the style you like, the motor should not be lower than 400W, otherwise the suction power will not be enough.


Bucket vacuum cleaner: There are two points when choosing a bucket vacuum cleaner. The power is generally 1000-1400W. The greater the power, the greater the suction power, and the volume of the bucket. The technical difficulty of the bucket vacuum cleaner is not high, so you don’t need to buy too expensive when purchasing. Because the bucket vacuum cleaner uses a wet and dry motor, the noise will be much louder than an ordinary motor.


Cordless handheld vacuum cleaner: Cordless handheld vacuum cleaner is currently the most mainstream in the industry, and it is also a battleground for various brands. The price also ranges from 5,000 for Dyson to more than 500 for domestic products. So how should you choose? Cordless handheld vacuum cleaner The core of the cleaner is a high-speed brushless motor with a speed ranging from 60,000 to 160,000 rpm. The higher the speed, the stronger the motor performance, the higher the cost, and the greater the power consumption. Generally, a motor of 80,000 rpm is enough, and a higher speed motor cannot actually exert its full power. Similar to the Dyson V12, the maximum speed is 120,000 rpm, but it can only be used at the highest speed for less than 10 minutes before shutting down. For normal use, it is still adjusted to the normal gear.


Some cheap cordless handheld vacuum cleaners use ordinary carbon brush motors with a rotation speed of about 20,000 rpm, and their performance is much weaker. The price is also relatively low.


Mite remover: The ultraviolet lamp of the mite remover has no effect, because the lamp needs to be stationary in one place for about an hour to have a sterilizing effect. The real function is the flapping function and the vacuum motor. The currently popular double-barrel mite remover is more than twice the price of the single-barrel mite remover, and it actually has no special effect. In the early years, double-barrel or even three-barrel whirlwind models also appeared on horizontal vacuum cleaners for a while, but because they had no practical effect, the mainstream later became single-barrel.

Just choose a single-barrel mite remover. The wired one should cost less than 300 yuan, and the wireless one should cost less than 600 yuan.



Suction at will: If you suction at will, it is basically just a point. Choose a brushless motor, otherwise you will find that slightly larger garbage cannot be sucked in.

Dyson: James Dyson, the former richest man in the UK, the owner of the most private land in the UK, the father of the cyclone vacuum cleaner, and the father of the wireless smart vacuum cleaner. The two most important functions in the vacuum cleaner industry at present were invented by Dyson: the cyclone system and the wireless high-speed motor (called brushless motor in the industry). In the past few years, Dyson’s vacuum cleaner also had the highest sales, the most expensive price and the best performance in the entire industry. Dyson is in the vacuum cleaner industry. The position in the cleaner industry is somewhat similar to Apple's position in the mobile phone industry. In the past two years, competitors have caught up, so Dyson's advantage is not as great as before. Some subsequent products also have many deviations in the technical route, and no one in the industry pays much attention to it.


Dyson's advantage lies in the brushless motor. In the early days, it was basically 2-3 years ahead of the industry. Now the difference in the parameters of the optical motor is not too big. However, among high-priced brands, Dyson is still more recommended than other brands such as Electrolux and Miele.



Dreame: Dreame is a Xiaomi ecosystem company. Starting from 2019, Dreame has made a big breakthrough in cleaning appliances.
