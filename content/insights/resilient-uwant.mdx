Yesterday Uwant held a product launch event and released its latest vacuum cleaner product: the Uwant Saodisheng vacuum cleaner.


Uwant Saodisheng is a station-based floor-sweeping vacuum cleaner, and it includes many innovations.

First, the HEPA filter and steel mesh are self-cleaning. There is an automatic dust-scraping device. The floor brush has green light dust detection. After the vacuum cleaner is used, it can be pushed directly into the station, and the station sucks the debris from the vacuum cleaner into the station. This allows the vacuum cleaner to go dozens of days without cleaning.

The biggest innovation is in the floor-brush design. It changes the traditional rotating roller inside the floor brush into a front-and-back vibrating roller body, avoiding floor-brush tangling at the source.

At this launch event, there were many tributes to Dyson. In station-based vacuum cleaners, domestic brands have already comprehensively surpassed Dyson.

At the launch site, Uwant invited Li Weijia, a host from Hunan TV. Li Weijia has long been known online for being a cleanliness obsessive. When he goes home, he not only cleans every area, but even cleans the soles of his shoes. Such a celebrity image fits vacuum cleaners very well.

It is said that Uwant can reach RMB 4 billion in sales this year. This result itself is impressive, not to mention that it is in the cleaning-appliance industry surrounded by giants. I said in previous videos that the cleaning-appliance industry has been hell-level difficulty in recent years. Hundreds of billions of RMB in capital entered the industry, and countless powerful companies suffered major setbacks here. Uwant surviving in such an environment and growing into a giant with several billions of RMB in sales is very impressive.

Here I will also talk about some information I know about Uwant.

Mr. Yang Yongbo of Uwant comes from e-commerce marketing. Previously, he was Bissell's domestic agent, and Bissell's strong period in China was also operated by their team.

During the cleaning-appliance boom in 2020, Mr. Yang and his team also decided to establish their own brand. At the very beginning, Uwant only had the English brand name Uwant. The Chinese name later came into use in the following years.

The first product was a dual-cup mite remover. The mite-remover category had already become a very common product, with retail prices around RMB 199 for years. But Uwant launched a dual-cup mite remover at RMB 799, and sold hundreds of thousands of units a year, making peers very envious.

Then the entire industry followed the dual-cup mite-remover design, and now dual-cup mite removers have become mainstream. From actual product experience, the dual dust cup does not perform especially differently; cyclone separation sales can also be achieved with a single dust cup. But for consumers who do not understand the industry, dual cups are a very good marketing point. Uwant is a very strong marketing company and can grasp industry pain points well.

Next came fabric cleaners.

This product line previously existed mainly in the North American market. When Uwant previously acted as Bissell's agent, it sold very well. When it made this product line itself, it immediately made fabric cleaners explode in China. The whole industry followed. However, the domestic fabric cleaner market did not become hot, while some companies began selling very strongly overseas.

The popularity of the above two product lines was completely led by Uwant. In terms of price, Uwant was relatively high in the industry and did not fight price wars with others. As these two products became popular, Uwant also received investment from Sequoia.

Then came the hard floor washer war. Naturally, Uwant did not miss it and launched a product with dual rollers plus blade cutting.

At that time, the industry giants were companies with deep pockets such as Tineco, Dreame, Roborock, Haier and Midea. It was very difficult to compete with these companies in marketing promotion and products.

For a period, I was doing product livestream reviews and selling branded hard floor washers, and I often dealt with these brands. Uwant pioneered the official brand review-style livestream room for selling goods. At that time, it could probably achieve hundreds of thousands or even more than one million RMB in sales per day.

At present, Uwant's penetration on Xiaohongshu, Douyin and Bilibili should be the strongest. Both large and small bloggers probably receive their promotion requests. I have an account that I had not used for a long time, and I also received their promotion inquiry.

If you look at Uwant's product line, it does not have very flashy functions like Tineco and Dreame, nor does it have huge capital investment. It mostly makes some small improvements. Sometimes compared with competing products, it does not have many special advantages. But through its strong marketing and sales capability, it can sell very large volumes. This is also why it survived and grew in a cruel competitive environment.

As the product line keeps expanding, Uwant's R&D team is also growing. It now has several hundred people. Part of its products are self-produced, and part are produced by partner factories. Uwant's robot vacuum factory is now also doing OEM production for some overseas giants. I originally thought they might be able to reach around RMB 1 billion, but the figure I heard was that they could reach RMB 4 billion this year, which was far beyond my imagination.

Many friends often ask me how I view Uwant as a company.

I thought about it a lot, and I think one word may be most appropriate: resilient. Like weeds with strong vitality, no matter how harsh the external environment is, Mr. Yang and his team can always find suitable opportunities to survive and grow.

Whether in product R&D or marketing promotion, Uwant is a company worth seriously studying.
