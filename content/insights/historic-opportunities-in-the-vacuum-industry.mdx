## Historical Opportunities and Industry Giants in Cleaning Appliances


1. **Wireless Handheld Vacuum Cleaner**. In 2016, Dyson launched its wireless handheld vacuum cleaner, which significantly outperformed traditional models with its powerful digital motor (DDM) and lithium battery. Under the leadership of this product, Dyson's founder James Dyson became one of Britain’s richest men, and Dyson vacuum cleaners rose to become the most recognizable brand in the cleaning appliance industry.

2. **Laser Navigation Robot Vacuum Cleaners**. During iRobot's heyday, Roborock achieved sales of hundreds of thousands of units within its first year, successfully going public and reaching a market valuation of over 10 billion yuan. Currently, Roborock is the global leader in robot vacuum cleaner sales. Other industry giants include Aiper Intelligent from Shenzhen and Cecotec from Spain.

3. **Wet-Dry All-In-One Robot Vacuum Cleaners**. In 2018, Narwal introduced a wet-dry all-in-one robot vacuum cleaner. With the rise of the pandemic and the popularity of platforms like Douyin and Xiaohongshu, Narwal became a unicorn in the industry with a valuation reaching 3 billion yuan. This marked the beginning of the era of water-station equipped robot vacuums.

4. **Hard Floor Washers**. Prior to 2020, Tineco under Ecovacs was an unremarkable domestic brand, and its first hard floor washer received little attention. However, with the pandemic and support from platforms like Douyin, Ecovacs' hard floor washers experienced explosive growth, leading to a significant increase in stock prices and creating industry miracles. The success of Tineco sparked a nationwide trend for hard floor washers, attracting billions of investment into the previously low-margin market.

5. **All-In-One Dock Robot Vacuum Cleaners**. While Narwal pioneered the era of water-station equipped robot vacuums, Ecovacs combined dust collection and wet stations in 2021 with its X1 all-in-one dock. The product was a huge success, selling hundreds of thousands of units that year. Over the next two years, Ecovacs dominated the market for robot vacuum cleaners. By 2024, overseas markets also saw strong sales of all-in-one docks, with Roborock and Ecovacs pushing out most foreign brands through these products, generating substantial profits.

6. **Mechanical Arm Robot Vacuum Cleaners**. This year, Roborock launched the G30 Space exploration version, a groundbreaking product that elevates robot vacuum cleaning from two-dimensional to three-dimensional. Although this product may not achieve high sales volumes, it marks the beginning of a new product form in the industry.
