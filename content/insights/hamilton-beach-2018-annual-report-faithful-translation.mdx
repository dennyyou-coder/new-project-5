Hamilton Beach was first established by Louis Hamilton and Chester Beach in 1900. In 1990, NACCO acquired the Hamilton Beach Company from Glen Dimplex.

In 2017, NACCO split Hamilton Beach Company and listed it independently in the United States with the stock market code HBB.

![Original image 01](/images/insights/hamilton-beach-2018-annual-report-faithful-translation-image-01.png)

At present, Hamilton Beach Company mainly operates small household appliances business, and the overall business is divided into two major parts:

Hamilton Beach Brands (small appliance brand) and Kitchen Collen (kitchen appliance sales channel/store)
