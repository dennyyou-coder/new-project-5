Recently, a friend shared a Kickstarter product featuring a robotic vacuum cleaner with me, saying it had some very notable highlights.

![Original image 1](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-001.jpg)

The slogan states that Narwal is the world's first robot mop & vacuum featuring innovative self-cleaning technology.

![Original image 2](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-002.jpg)

![Original image 3](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-003.jpg)

The core self-cleaning functionality is achieved through 2 water tanks, one for dirty water and one for clean water. Then the robot automatically runs on the base station, with raised slots on the tray that clean the stains where the cloth contacts.

![Original image 4](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-004.jpg)

![Original image 5](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-005.jpg)

![Original image 6](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-006.jpg)

The cleaning effect of a mop that simultaneously automatically rotates is significantly stronger than that of current market-standard floor cleaning robots. It can clean dry red wine stains and markermarks, among others.

![Original image 7](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-007.jpg)

The motor is simultaneously configured with 18,000 PA, cleaning and suctioning during mopping.

![Original image 8](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-008.jpg)

Uses a laser navigation solution.

![Original image 9](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-009.jpg)

Set up virtual walls to prevent the robot from getting stuck in carpets and other areas.

![Original image 10](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-010.jpg)

Noise as low as 45 dB

![Original image 11](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-011.jpg)

The overall design is as follows, and it can be seen that the design is also very simple, on par with the designs from the Xiaomi series.

![Original image 12](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-012.jpg)

The crowdfunding price is $549 USD. This product's retail price should also be relatively high.

![Original image 13](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-013.jpg)

![Original image 14](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-014.jpg)

The personnel from Roborock also listed out some project timelines. I inquired about this project on the side and found that it indeed required considerable effort.

![Original image 15](/images/insights/narwal-and-the-self-cleaning-robot-vacuum-image-015.jpg)

To date, it has raised 780,000 US dollars through crowdfunding.
