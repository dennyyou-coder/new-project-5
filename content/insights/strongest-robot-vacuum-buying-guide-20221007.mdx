2. Navigation method of robot vacuum

Currently, in terms of navigation methods, robot vacuums are mainly divided into random navigation, inertial gyroscope navigation, LiDAR navigation, and visual navigation.


Random type: To put it simply, it means turning around randomly. You may scan some places many times in a day, and some places may not be scanned even once. In the early days, people's impression of robot vacuums was that artificial intelligence was retarded. The main reason was such a random type of machine. Nowadays, random type machines are basically not sold much on e-commerce platforms. Where they may appear, there are only some gift channels.


Inertial gyroscope navigation: Because the random type is unreliable, the use of inertial gyroscopes for positioning and navigation was later developed. Through various sensors and gyroscopes for positioning, relatively accurate path planning and navigation is achieved. The disadvantage is that the accuracy of gyroscope navigation is relatively low. There will still be missed scans. The advantage is that the cost of gyro navigation is relatively low, so among the robot vacuums exported from abroad, the ones with gyroscopes are the most common. The top of the main machine is bare and has no laser head or camera. It is a gyroscope or random navigation.

![The Strongest Robot Vacuum Buying Guide image 1](/images/insights/strongest-robot-vacuum-buying-guide-20221007-image-001.png)


LiDAR navigation: LiDAR navigation is currently the most accurate navigation method. The principle is to use a LiDAR sensor to quickly scan the entire house and then create a map. With an accurate map, the brain of the robot vacuum can accurately plan a reasonable and efficient sweeping plan. Generally, high-end robot vacuums are equipped with LiDAR navigation, which is also easy to identify from the appearance. There is a raised LiDAR module on the surface of the machine. The leader in the industry, Roborock Technology, had a LiDAR navigation product that only sold for 1,500 yuan. It detonated the entire market. Currently, Roborock Technology's products are basically LiDAR navigation.

![The Strongest Robot Vacuum Buying Guide image 2](/images/insights/strongest-robot-vacuum-buying-guide-20221007-image-002.png)


Visual navigation: Visual navigation is also another major genre of high-precision navigation, which uses cameras to scan the entire house and then build a map. The disadvantage of the camera is that there may be some problems in places with poor lighting. The overall accuracy is slightly worse than that of lasers, and the cost is slightly lower. The industry leader iRobot only uses visual navigation solutions, while OEMs like Ecovacs, Xiaomi or other OEMs have both visual and laser solutions.

![The Strongest Robot Vacuum Buying Guide image 3](/images/insights/strongest-robot-vacuum-buying-guide-20221007-image-003.png)

5. How to choose a robot vacuum

What points should you pay attention to when buying a robot vacuum?

1. Room size? If the room does not exceed 70 square meters, it is not recommended. Because if the room is too small, the robot vacuum cannot be deployed due to the household furnishings. At the same time, it is easy for the robot vacuum to get stuck in small places.


2. How to choose the suction power of a robot vacuum? The minimum suction power of a robot vacuum needs to be above 2KPA, so that most of the dry garbage on the ground can be swept in. But for carpets, the suction power is not enough. So Dyson increases the suction motor, but the problem is that the overall thickness is relatively large, and places such as the bottom of the bed and the bottom of the sofa cannot get in.

![The Strongest Robot Vacuum Buying Guide image 10](/images/insights/strongest-robot-vacuum-buying-guide-20221007-image-010.png)


3. Navigation method. As mentioned above among the four navigation methods, LiDAR navigation has the highest accuracy. With high measurement accuracy, more intelligent operations can be carried out in the future. So try to choose LiDAR navigation. High-end models generally use LiDAR navigation, and at least you have to choose visual navigation.


4. Is automatic water supply and drainage necessary? This function was first proposed by Narwal on J2. This function is also controversial. I will list the advantages and disadvantages for you, and you can choose according to the situation.


The advantage of filling and discharging water is that the water filling of the hard floor washer and the treatment of the sewage tank are completed automatically, so it can be done without any sense. However, the dust box of the robot vacuum is relatively small, and it usually has to be cleaned once every 1-2 days. In this regard, it is not necessary. If it is a machine with dust collection function, the dust bag should be cleaned once every two months. In this case, the water going up and down will be truly imperceptible.


In addition, the location requirements for the installation of water supply and drainage are relatively high. Generally, the robot vacuum is placed in the living room. If it is kept in the living room, it will need to add water lines, and the water pipe wiring will not be very beautiful.

If you want to move it to a place close to a water source, such as a bathroom, balcony laundry pool, etc., it may not be very convenient for the robot vacuum to work.
