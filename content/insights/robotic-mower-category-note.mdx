### Industry Report on Lawn Mowing Robots (As of May 2025)

![robotic mower category](/images/insights/robotic-mower-category-note-image-01.jpg)

#### I. Market Size and Growth

1. **Global Market**

   - In 2024, the market size reached $2.529 billion (+16%), with a penetration rate below 15%, and over 70% share in Europe and America.
   
2. **Future Projections**

   - By 2030, the market is expected to reach between $4-4.5 billion, with significant development potential in the U.S. market.

3. **Chinese Market**

   - In 2024, the market size was RMB 1.859 billion (+18.06%), with a notable increase in export business.
   
4. **Key Players**

   - Ninebot (annual revenue of RMB 861 million, +284%)
   - Ecovacs (overseas revenue increased by 186.7%)

#### II. Technological Evolution

| Technology Type | Cost | Precision | Representative Solutions | Suitable Scenarios |
|:---------------|:----|:---------|:-----------------------|:------------------|
| Wired | $600-$1,000 | Low | Electromagnetic boundary wire | Small family lawns |
| RTK + Vision | $1,000-$2,000 | ±2.5cm | Ninebot Navimow | Medium complex terrains |
| LiDAR | Over $2,000 | ±1cm | Ecovacs GOAT G1 | High-end commercial scenarios |
| Network RTK | Monthly rental of RMB 20-30 | ±1cm | Guanghutong solution | Large municipal green spaces |

**Trends:** Products without boundaries account for over 60% of the market, with navigation solutions upgrading to "RTK/LiDAR + 3D vision."

#### III. Industrial Chain Landscape

1. **Upstream**

   - Domestic replacement: Beiyun Technology (RTK modules), Horizon (chips), Xinyuanda (batteries)

2. **Midstream**

3. **Traditional Giants**

   - Husqvarna (wired systems)
   - Positec (Worx market share first)

4. **New Entrants**

   - Ninebot (price competition)
   - Ecovacs (technological diversity)
   - Dreame (rapid iteration)

#### IV. Risks and Challenges

1. **Technical Bottlenecks**

   - 10% residual grass at the edge of mowing, prone to slipping on slopes over 26°, and ineffective positioning under tree canopies.

2. **Business Risks**

   - Shenghe Innovation, a startup, went bankrupt due to delivery issues, reflecting an industry attrition rate exceeding 30%.

#### V. Investment Hotspots

1. **Capital Movements**

   - Positec received $250 million investment from CATL and Xpeng, with a valuation over RMB 15 billion.

2. **Potential Areas**

   - Integrated garden management (snow removal/watering)
   - Photovoltaic field weed control
   - Golf course maintenance
