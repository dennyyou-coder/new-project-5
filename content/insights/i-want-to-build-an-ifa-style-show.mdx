Yes, you did not read that wrong. I wrote that I want to build an IFA-style exhibition, not that I want to attend IFA.


As China's strength continues to grow, it is only a matter of time before Chinese brands become world-class brands. At that time, foreigners from around the world will gather in Suzhou and Shanghai to look for business cooperation opportunities. I want to build a Chinese version of IFA, so Chinese brands can release their new products on their own home field.

Some people may hear this and say: Denny, has your brain burned out? You are a tiny company. If you attend IFA and get a small booth, that is already good enough. You still want to build a Chinese version of IFA? Do you know how difficult this is?

At the recent IFA show, all the cleaning-appliance giants released all kinds of impressive black technologies. IFA could almost be directly renamed the Chinese Brands Show.

Midea boldly booked an entire hall at IFA, turning the exhibition into its own private launch event.

Dreame took nearly a 1,000-square-meter booth and released stair-climbing robots, robotic mowers and full-scenario products.

MOVA also released stair-climbing robots, robotic mower products and various other black-technology products.

Roborock placed a giant advertisement outside the hall, and in addition to cleaning-appliance products, it released three robotic mowers in one breath.

Eufy under Anker also released a stair-climbing robot with a high degree of completion. It is said to be officially launched in 2026.

By contrast, exhibitors from other countries basically had little movement. The original spaces of Samsung, LG and others were replaced by Chinese giants. In recent years, without the efforts of Chinese brands, IFA would not have much to watch.

I have also attended IFA several times before. The difference between us and these giants is that as an OEM manufacturer, we did not go to the brand hall. The organizer probably disliked these OEM manufacturers for not being high-end enough, but still wanted to make money from them, so it directly created a China hall in a remote place and put these Chinese manufacturers together. The popularity of this China hall was very poor. Over a three-day exhibition, one might collect only 20 to 30 business cards.

The value of IFA is that it is Europe's largest home-appliance exhibition. Because transportation is convenient, many buyers from surrounding countries come. For Chinese brands entering the European market, IFA is a good publicity and launch channel. It can demonstrate strength very well and allow direct communication with buyers, quickly advancing cooperation.

In addition to European-market customers, practitioners from many other countries also come here to look for opportunities. The U.S. CES show follows the same logic.

But distance is not the only consideration. Business opportunity is. Every year, foreigners from around the world come to Guangzhou to attend the Canton Fair and look for suitable business opportunities.

As I just said, IFA's actual effect is ordinary, but other overseas exhibitions are even worse. In 2023, we attended ten exhibitions globally. Once, we attended a show in Italy, and the result was painfully bad. Over three days, we collected only five or six business cards, and after contacting them later there was no response at all.

I have been in this industry for almost 20 years. Over the years, I have attended hundreds of large and small professional exhibitions around the world. My feeling is that most organizers are not professional. They do not understand the industry and do not think for exhibitors. Their only concern is selling booths. Once booths are sold, they do not care whether exhibitors live or die. Exhibitors who attend such shows once will not attend a second time. If there are no exhibitors, buyers are even less likely to come. So some exhibitions are still very poor in quality even after being held for many years.

So what kind of exhibition will I build?

What problems can I solve?

What benefits can I bring to everyone?

I will build a specialized cleaning-appliance exhibition, including home cleaning, such as vacuum cleaners, hard floor washers and robot vacuums; yard cleaning, such as pool cleaning and robotic mowers; commercial and medical cleaning; automotive cleaning and more. Exhibitors will include brand owners, complete-machine OEMs, component suppliers and solution providers. The date has not yet been set, but it is tentatively planned for March to May 2026 and will last three days.

For exhibitors, what they want most is business cooperation opportunities. Different exhibitors have different target customer groups.

For brand exhibitors, the main purpose of attending is to promote their products and attract more dealers and channel partners for cooperation. So I will find ways to contact various streamer channels, buyers from major e-commerce platforms and retail stores, and dealers, inviting them to attend.

For OEM complete-machine exhibitors, they want to connect with domestic and overseas brand customers. I will invite buyers and R&D teams from major domestic brands. For some customers with purchasing offices in China, I will also invite them. I will also email overseas customers, though in the short term, foreigners may still need some time to get used to attending Chinese exhibitions other than the Canton Fair.

For component suppliers, the procurement and R&D teams of brands and complete-machine factories are their customers. I will invite a large number of industry professionals to visit, and I will also organize dedicated procurement and sales matchmaking.

For professional visitors, their goals are roughly as follows.

For procurement and R&D people, they hope to see the latest products released by industry giants, find new products and higher-quality suppliers, and also access new supply-chain technologies that can later be used in their own products.

If they can meet most suppliers in the industry in one day, all procurement and R&D people will be willing to attend such an exhibition.

In addition to a regular exhibition, I will also hold an industry forum at the same time.

I will invite capable industry leaders from various areas to share. Our previous sharing sessions in Suzhou and Shenzhen each had more than 500 paying participants, which also shows that our content is very practical and valuable.

In addition, we will simultaneously hold targeted procurement meetings for brand owners, allowing supply chains and brands to connect more efficiently.

The above is my preliminary idea. Of course, many details still need to be implemented. In any case, we will definitely be more professional and more valuable than existing exhibition organizers.

I reported this matter to several good friends and industry leaders. Some good friends directly confirmed they would exhibit. Mr. Yu from Dreame also said he would strongly support it. In addition, some industry leaders raised questions and later asked me to come over for further discussion.

I am very grateful for the support of these good friends and industry leaders. They supported me personally without having any information at all. I will not let everyone down. Once I decide to do it, I must do it better than others.

My future transformation direction is already relatively clear: shifting from product development to serving the entire industry. In addition to the exhibition, I also have a product company that can reach tens of millions of RMB in sales this year. The future direction of this company is to help factories in the cleaning-appliance industry sell products to customers around the world. Product sales are no longer about relying purely on information gaps to earn huge windfall profits, but about earning hard money through differences in technology and service.

I may also do cleaning-appliance sourcing for some domestic and overseas customers, providing a full chain from product development, communication, sampling and inspection to delivery. Because of our relationships with factories, we may also help customers obtain better prices. In the complex environment of cleaning appliances, our goal is to find the most suitable products for customers and help customers make money.

In any case, my future will have countless directions. If everyone has any ideas, you are welcome to communicate with me.
