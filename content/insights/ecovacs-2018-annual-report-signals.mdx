The next direction for development is sensors. Tineco's latest wireless vacuum cleaners have already begun using a dust sensor to automatically adjust suction power.

![Original image 5](/images/insights/ecovacs-2018-annual-report-signals-image-005.jpg)

Revenue in 2018 had already reached RMB 5.694 billion, representing a growth of 25.11%. At this rate, Ecowech is expected to surpass Kingclean and become the top brand in the vacuum cleaner industry in 2019.

![Original image 6](/images/insights/ecovacs-2018-annual-report-signals-image-006.jpg)

Financial data for each quarter

![Original image 7](/images/insights/ecovacs-2018-annual-report-signals-image-007.jpg)

The data for each segment shows that the gross margin for small appliances (vacuums) is indeed too low, at only 17%, while service robots have a margin of 47.57%.
