Danish industrial cleaning equipment giant Nilfisk (Lirchi) released its annual report for 2018, and I have briefly excerpted some of the information for reference.


Sales in 2018 amounted to 1.054 billion euros, with operating profit increasing by 11.5% compared to 2017.

![Original image 1](/images/insights/nilfisk-2018-annual-report-commercial-cleaning-image-001.jpg)

![Original image 2](/images/insights/nilfisk-2018-annual-report-commercial-cleaning-image-002.jpg)

In 2017, the following parts were separated:

Noridic Chemical & Utensils with an annual turnover of approximately €10 million

Nilfisk South Africa with an annual turnover of about €13 million

Turkish high-pressure cleaning machine manufacturer Rottest, with an annual turnover of around €1 million

U.S. carpet cleaning business Hydramaster, with an annual turnover exceeding €15 million

Nilfisk Outdook (outdoor business) with an annual turnover exceeding €35 million.

![Original image 3](/images/insights/nilfisk-2018-annual-report-commercial-cleaning-image-003.jpg)

Closed 7 factories:

United States Mukilteo factory (Hydramaster business), Italian Guardamiglio factory (outdoor product business), Turkish Izmir factory (high-pressure cleaning machines), South African Johannesburg factory, Chinese Suzhou factory business transferred to Dongguan, Singapore Den-sin factory.

![Original image 4](/images/insights/nilfisk-2018-annual-report-commercial-cleaning-image-004.jpg)

Sales in 2018 were 1.0543 billion euros, 2017 was 1.0819 billion euros, 2016 was 1.0585 billion euros, 2015 was 0.980 billion euros, and 2014 was 0.9176 billion euros.

![Original image 5](/images/insights/nilfisk-2018-annual-report-commercial-cleaning-image-005.jpg)

Sales by Product:

Floor Care: €347.6 million, approximately 33%
Vacuum Cleaners: €207.6 million, approximately 20%
High-Pressure Washers: €167.6 million, approximately 16%
Aftermarket: €331.5 million, approximately 31%

![Original image 6](/images/insights/nilfisk-2018-annual-report-commercial-cleaning-image-006.jpg)

![Original image 7](/images/insights/nilfisk-2018-annual-report-commercial-cleaning-image-007.jpg)

Sales breakdown by region:

EMEA 46%, North America 26%, APAC 7%, professional products 12%, consumer products 9%.

![Original image 8](/images/insights/nilfisk-2018-annual-report-commercial-cleaning-image-008.jpg)

Gross margin by region:

EMEA 46%, North America 25%, APAC 7%, professional products 14%, consumer products 8%.

![Original image 9](/images/insights/nilfisk-2018-annual-report-commercial-cleaning-image-009.jpg)

![Original image 10](/images/insights/nilfisk-2018-annual-report-commercial-cleaning-image-010.jpg)

EMEA sales amounted to 487 million euros, with a natural growth rate of 1.2%, accounting for 46% of total sales. The gross margin was 41.8%. Core markets include Germany, France, UK, Denmark, and Sweden. In terms of product sales in the EMEA region, Floorcare accounted for 32%, vacuum cleaners for 32%, pressure washers for 14%, and after-sales services for 20%.

![Original image 11](/images/insights/nilfisk-2018-annual-report-commercial-cleaning-image-011.jpg)

North American regional sales amounted to 270 million euros, with a natural growth rate of -0.3%, accounting for 26% of total sales. The gross margin was 41.9%. Core markets included the United States, Canada, and Mexico. Product sales in the North American region were distributed as follows: Floorcare accounted for 52%, vacuum cleaners for 7%, pressure washers for 13%, and after-sales market for 28%.

![Original image 12](/images/insights/nilfisk-2018-annual-report-commercial-cleaning-image-012.jpg)

APAC region sales amounted to 79 million euros, with a natural growth rate of 1.7%, accounting for 7% of total sales. The gross margin was 42%. Core markets: Australia, China, Singapore, Thailand. Product sales in the APAC region by category were as follows: Floorcare accounted for 37%, vacuum cleaners for 18%, pressure washers for 13%, and after-sales market for 32%.

![Original image 13](/images/insights/nilfisk-2018-annual-report-commercial-cleaning-image-013.jpg)

Professional-grade product sales amounted to 124 million euros, with a natural growth rate of 11.4%, accounting for 12% of total sales. The gross margin was 48.8%. Product sales breakdown: IVS 56%, Nilfisk food 14%, high-pressure washers 19%, and Hydramaster 11%.

![Original image 14](/images/insights/nilfisk-2018-annual-report-commercial-cleaning-image-014.jpg)

Sales of consumer-grade products amounted to 95 million euros, with a natural growth rate of 1.4%, accounting for 9% of total sales. The gross margin was 34.1%. Sales breakdown by product category: pressure washers at 56%, vacuum cleaners at 35%, and other at 9%.

Target for 2019:

Professional-grade products under the Nilfisk brand experienced natural growth of 3%, consumer-grade products saw no natural growth, private label products showed a natural decline of 10%, resulting in overall business growth of 2%, with EBITA increasing by 14.4%.
