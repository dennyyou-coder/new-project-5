In 1994, Ni Zuguang left Chunhua Motor Factory and founded the predecessor of Lixie Electrical, Suzhou Jinleke Appliances, which was named Kingclean in English, meaning "Clean King." Over the following years, Jinleke became a dominant contract manufacturer in the vacuum cleaner industry. In 2015, Lixie Electrical successfully went public, living up to its name as Clean King.


One of the earliest foreign trade professionals in the Chinese vacuum cleaner industry, Qian Dongqi, established his own vacuum cleaner factory, TEK Appliances (Suzhou) Co., Ltd., in 1998. Around 2006, Ecovacs entered the robot vacuum market, experiencing a series of challenging product development phases and the rise of e-commerce. In 2018, Ecovacs successfully went public.

These three industry leaders represent different directions:

Leku Electrical is a traditional vacuum cleaner manufacturer and has long been the leader in the vacuum cleaner industry.

Ecovacs Robotics has benefited from the explosive growth of robot vacuums and currently represents that segment of the market.

Roborock, as a company representing Xiaomi's extreme cost-performance strategy, has also benefited from this approach, with single product sales exceeding 1 billion RMB.

**So, which of these three companies will emerge as the "King of Cleanliness" in the long term?**

From a sales perspective, Kingclean achieved revenues of 5.8 billion yuan in 2018, making it the highest among the three companies currently. A deeper analysis reveals that Kingclean's growth rate for 2018 was only 2%. Of its 5.8 billion yuan in revenue, over 4 billion yuan came from contract manufacturing, while less than 1.5 billion yuan came from its own brands. Analyzing the contract manufacturing clients within the vacuum cleaner industry, it appears that Kingclean has already become the largest player in this segment and is unlikely to see significant growth going forward. Therefore, we expect limited business growth for Kingclean in 2019.

Ecovacs reported revenues of 5.6 billion yuan in 2018, marking a 25.11% increase from the previous year. Assuming Ecovacs grows by another 20% in 2019, its sales for that year would reach approximately 6.7 billion yuan, surpassing Kingclean. Currently, Ecovacs' contract manufacturing business has significantly reduced compared to its own brands, which account for a larger portion of the company's revenue.

Roborock generated revenues of 3 billion yuan in 2018. Assuming a natural growth rate of 20%, Roborock is expected to reach approximately 3.8 billion yuan in 2019. In 2018, Roborock launched its new cordless handheld vacuum cleaner priced at 1,299 yuan, with an estimated sales volume of 200,000 to 300,000 units per year, contributing nearly 300 million yuan in revenue. A reasonable estimate suggests that Roborock's total sales for 2019 will be around 4 billion yuan.

### Profit Margins

Ecovacs > Roborock > Liqitai. The substantial amount of contract manufacturing by Liqitai has dragged down its overall profit margins. Ecovacs has benefited significantly from the development of robot vacuums both domestically and internationally, leading to a significant increase in sales and profits. One reason for Roborock's lower profit margin compared to Ecovacs is the low-margin model of Xiaomi. Balancing this aspect will be key for Roborock's future growth.

### External Competitive Environment

#### Liqitai Electrical:

In 2019, Liqitai faced significant competition from industry rivals such as TEK, Midea, Appliance, and Chunju. Additionally, due to the North American tariff issues, it had to establish factories in Southeast Asia, adding another layer of uncertainty to its external environment. The leading advantage in contract manufacturing that Liqitai once held is gradually being eroded by other companies.

Domestically, the Magic Clean series from Liqitai has not performed well in sales. Its unique design did not become mainstream over time and instead faced increasing competition as more companies entered the market, putting it at risk of becoming marginalized.

#### Ecovacs:

Ecovacs holds a significant leading position in the domestic robot vacuum cleaner industry; only Roborock poses a threat to its dominance. Besides brand recognition, Ecovacs' technology also leads other companies.

Ecovacs has performed well with its own-brand business in the international market and launched the new Tineco (Tineco) brand to replicate the success of its own brand. The current issue is intense competition for cordless vacuum cleaners; currently, neither Tineco nor TEK can stand out in the domestic market. However, Tineco's cordless vacuum cleaners have already achieved good sales on Amazon US.

#### Roborock:

Roborock stands out uniquely in the domestic market with just three products generating 3 billion yuan in revenue, truly embodying the strategy of extreme single-item/best-selling product. However, it is important to note that the external environment at the time of introducing robot vacuums was a key factor. When Roborock launched its LDS LiDAR robot vacuum for 1,699 RMB, the market average price was between 3,000-4,000 RMB, allowing it to dominate the market and pose significant threats to Ecovacs.

However, with competitors like Ecovacs adjusting their prices to be only 100-200 RMB higher than Roborock's, the price advantage is no longer as pronounced. Additionally, Roborock recently launched a cordless handheld vacuum cleaner priced at 1,299 RMB; this price was not competitive in April 2019. If it had been released earlier, around late 2017 or early 2018, there would have been a high likelihood of replicating the success of robot vacuums. The pursuit of extreme performance has also impacted Roborock's listing.

Currently, Roborock still holds a clear competitive advantage in robot vacuum cleaners but faces intense competition from multiple brands for its handheld vacuum cleaner. In international markets, many foreigners are reverse-buying robot vacuums; the overseas market could be an important point of growth for Roborock.

### Talent Reserves

#### Liqitai:

Liqitai has sent out more people to start businesses in the industry than any other company, earning it the nickname "Huazhong University." Due to its leading position in research and development, Liqitai's R&D department is of great concern to Ni Zuguang. The industry also favors Liqitai's employees for their ability to work on various tasks.

#### Ecovacs:

Ecovacs has a detailed job distribution corresponding to different positions. As the founder comes from sales, Ecovacs is sensitive to market trends and follows the latest industry hotspots in product development without being constrained by specific designs; market priority is key.

#### Roborock:

Roborock stands out as an unique manufacturing company, having recruited many employees from Huawei, IBM, Tencent, etc., rather than traditional vacuum cleaner manufacturers. This has created a corporate culture more akin to a tech company than a manufacturing one.

### Risks and Threats

#### Liqitai:

The risk of contract manufacturing lies in the relationship with major clients. Additionally, during the lithium-ion battery era, Liqitai's technological advantage was not as significant as before.

The biggest challenge for its own-brand business is the positioning of the Magic Clean series, which has unrealistically high pricing (3-4,000 RMB) and an unmarketable design, leading to reduced sales. If no strong products or more reasonable pricing are introduced in the next two years, Liqitai's domestic market will quickly be eroded by other brands.

#### Ecovacs:

Ecovacs' biggest risk is the pursuit of Xiaomi, but this competition has also prompted Ecovacs to reconsider its positioning and adjust its product lines. Currently, Ecovacs still holds a clear leading position in robot vacuum cleaners. The weakness may lie with the new Tineco brand for cordless vacuum cleaners; while the market is larger, competition is fierce.

#### Roborock:

Roborock's extreme pursuit of product perfection could also lead to missing important time windows. Whether future products can match the success of the robot vacuum cleaner will be a significant test for Roborock.

### Conclusion

Over the next 2-3 years, I am more optimistic about Ecovacs becoming the new leader in cleaning appliances. Over a longer period of 4-5 years, Roborock is also a strong contender.
