1.Samsung Powerstick Jet

Samsung has released the Power Stick Jet, a new cordless vacuum cleaner for the first time. It looks a bit like the Dyson V10 in appearance. A series of domestic factories have already taken the lead in making this style. From this aspect, in the future, cordless handheld vacuum cleaners will continue to follow the V10 style.

2.Miele Triflex


German home appliance giant Miele has finally released its cordless handheld vacuum cleaner. Previously, as a representative of high-end dust bag vacuum cleaners in the vacuum cleaner industry, Miele has always been a strong competitor to Dyson (Dyson previously only had bagless models). Three years after the vacuum cleaner industry went cordless, Miele finally released the Triflex.

![Original image 006](/images/insights/ifa-2019-vacuum-cleaner-new-products-by-major-brands-image-006.png)


![Original image 007](/images/insights/ifa-2019-vacuum-cleaner-new-products-by-major-brands-image-007.png)

From a styling point of view, the biggest feature of Triflex is that the handle and main body can be reassembled into traditional Stick vacuum cleaners, Dyson style and small handheld vacuum cleaners. This is also the origin of the name Triflex. The price is also very beautiful, 699 pounds, and the front is just Dyson.

3.AEG QX9

AEG has released a rather special-looking vacuum cleaner, the QX9. Its main features are lightness and long battery life. The middle vacuum cleaner part can be taken out and used as an independent handheld vacuum cleaner. The price is 499 euros.
